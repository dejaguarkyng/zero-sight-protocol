# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume positive intent
- Give and receive feedback professionally
- Focus on what is best for the community and project

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Publishing private information without consent
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct. They may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Report Code of Conduct incidents to the maintainer privately. All reports will be reviewed and handled as confidentially as possible.
